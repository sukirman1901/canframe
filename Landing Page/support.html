Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why does my frame have a solid background instead of a hole?

A: Make sure you upload a PNG image that has a truly transparent background (alpha channel) where the hole should be. JPEGs do not support transparency.

Q: Does it work with complex shapes?

A: Yes! Our algorithm traces any transparent hole inside your image, no matter how complex the shape is.

Q: Does this app collect my images?

A: No. All image processing happens locally and directly within the Canva editor. We do not store or see your images.
