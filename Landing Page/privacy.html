Privacy Policy

The "Image to Frame" Canva App operates entirely locally within your browser using the Canva Apps SDK. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, images, or metadata to external servers.

All image processing (converting your images into frames) is done entirely on the client-side (in your browser). We do not have access to the images you upload.

3. Third-Party Services

As a Canva App, your use of the app is also governed by Canva's Privacy Policy. The App uses the Canva Apps SDK to interact with your Canva editor session.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
